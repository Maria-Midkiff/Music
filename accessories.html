Accessories

Stop by one of our stores for all of your accessory needs. We have a full supply of accessoires. Consult with our musicians to make sure you have the right set up. We can help you get the most out of your musical investment!

Some accessories include:

  • Guitar Accessories (picks, strings, straps, capos, stands, soft or hard cases, tuners, effects pedals, slides)
  • Piano/Keyboard Accessories (keyboard stand, sustain pedal, keyboard bench, dust cover, keyboard case or gig bag, MIDI controller, keyboard amplifier)
  • Bass Accessories (strings, pickups, preamp/DI box, gig bag or hard case, straps, bass effects pedals, bass tuner)
  • Wind and Brass Instruments (reeds, mouthpieces, ligatures, cleaning swabs or brushes, valve oil, cork grease
  • String Instruments (Violin, Viola, Cello, Double Bass) (rosin, shoulder rest, bow case, fine tuners, soft or hard case, endpin rest)
  • Drum Accessories (drumsticks, drum key, stool, practice pads, drum mat, silencing pads, hardware case, drumstick bag, cymbal stand extensions)
  • Recording and Studio Accessories (studio monitors, audio interface, pop filter, mic stand, soundproofing panels, studio headphones, DAW software)
  • General Accessories (metronome, music stand, cable ties, surge protectors, batteries, polishing supplies)
