Rentals

Our music store offers a variety of rental items to cater to the needs of musicians, beginners, and enthusiasts. Contact us so we can discuss your needs for any of the following:

Short- and Long-Term Rentals Avaliable

  1. Musical Instruments - guitars (acoustic, electric, bass), keyboards, pianos, drum sets, violins, cellos, saxophones, trumpets, clarinets, and flutes. You can try before purchase as well.
  2. Sound Equipment - PA systems, microphones, speakers, amplifiers, mixers, and audio recording gear. This is useful for events, performances, or recording sessions, where musicians and event organizers may not want to invest in expensive equipment for a one-time or occasional use.
  3. Music Stands and Accessories - music stands for rehearsals, performances, or music classes. We also offer rental options for accessories like instrument cases, guitar straps, metronomes, and tuners.
  4. Studio Practice Rooms - studio facilities for practice or rehearsal. These rooms are equipped with instruments and sound systems, providing a space for musicians to practice individually or rehearse with their bands.
  5. Sheet Music and Method Books - rental services for sheet music and method books. This is particularly helpful for students who need specific music pieces for a limited period or for musicians exploring new genres or styles without committing to purchasing full sheet music collections.
  6. Audio Visual Equipment - used in events and performances, audio visual equipment like projectors, screens, and lighting systems are available for rent.
