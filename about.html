The Founders

Lucy

Hailing from a musical family, Lucy discovered her passion for the flute at an early age, captivated by the instrument's enchanting tones and versatility. With years of dedication and relentless pursuit of excellence, she has evolved into a true flute virtuoso. Trained in both classical and contemporary styles, Lucy has honed her skills under the guidance of esteemed flutists and mentors, mastering the intricacies of embouchure, breath control, and musical expression. Her deep understanding of music theory and harmony allows her to breathe life into every note she plays, infusing each performance with emotion and soul. As a seasoned performer, Lucy has graced prestigious stages and concert halls around the world, captivating audiences with her heartfelt renditions and impeccable technique. She has collaborated with renowned orchestras, chamber ensembles, and contemporary bands.

Dan

Dan is a multi-talented artist who seamlessly bridges the worlds of music performance and production, leaving an indelible mark on the industry through his unique creative vision. With a profound love for both the art of sound and the technical intricacies of music production, Dan has carved a distinctive path in the ever-evolving landscape of modern music. As a musician, Dan is a virtuoso of various instruments, displaying a remarkable ability to convey emotions and tell stories through his soulful performances. From delicate melodies to powerful improvisations, his music transcends genres, blending influences from classical, jazz, and contemporary styles, captivating audiences with its sheer brilliance.

Mitch

Mitch is a gifted and dynamic guitar player whose melodic mastery knows no bounds, enchanting audiences with his expressive performances and exceptional musicality. With an unwavering passion for the guitar, Mitch has become a prominent figure in the world of music, captivating listeners with his unique style and unparalleled skill. From an early age, Mitch was drawn to the magic of the guitar's six strings. With relentless dedication and countless hours of practice, he honed his craft, mastering a wide range of techniques, from intricate fingerpicking to scintillating solos. His profound understanding of music theory allows him to effortlessly weave melodies that resonate with emotion and soul. A true virtuoso, Mitch's versatility spans across various genres, effortlessly transitioning from soulful blues to intricate jazz, and from electrifying rock to delicate classical compositions. His ability to infuse each genre with his signature touch sets him apart as an exceptional and sought-after guitarist.
